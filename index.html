About the Acura NSX

The Acura NSX (2nd generation, Honda NSX) is a 2-seater, all-wheel drive, mid-engine hybrid sports car developed and manufactured by Honda in the United States. It succeeds the original NSX that was produced in Japan from 1990 to 2005.

  • Dimensions (L x W x H, in inches): 176 x 76 x 47.8
  • Body Style: 2-door coupe
  • Electric Motor: Dual Front, Single Rear
